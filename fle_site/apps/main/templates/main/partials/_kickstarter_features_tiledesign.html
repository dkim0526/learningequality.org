Content Curation

Kolibri Studio takes open educational resource use to the next level by enabling alignment to local curricular standards, allowing educators to easily browse and select from libraries, and letting users compare materials from a variety of sources. Contact us for early access to Kolibri Studio

★ Build, re-order, and redesign your own library of digital resources. Using the Studio interface, an instructor can create a channel for students’ use from the existing library, for example: combining math content from Khan Academy, interactive physics simulations from PhET, and a story from African Storybook, easily integrate a variety of learning materials into their curriculum.

★ Upload new materials Whether they’re student-created products, quality sample lesson plans, or digitized local books, any local materials can easily be uploaded onto Kolibri Studio to be combined and supplemented with content from around the world.

★ Align to curriculum: With Studio’s channel structure, clipboard utility, search tools, and browsable topic trees, users have the tools to map open educational resources to their own curricular and learning objectives. Working personally with our partners, Learning Equality is also working to provide libraries pre-aligned to high-use standards, such as those of Mexico’s SEP and India’s NCERT.

Syncing and Sharing

Devices running Kolibri will be able to find and directly connect with each other over a local Wi-Fi network, allowing for easy content sharing and data updates.

Packaged for offline use

We developed special processes to package online learning materials to be taken offline. One of our innovations is a video compression technique that convert the pen strokes and objects in a blackboard-style video into scalable vector objects.

In our tests, a 250MB video can now be compressed into just a single megabyte, while retaining most of the original quality. This reduces the time and cost needed to download a large set of videos, and allows a large amount of content to be stored on a small, low-cost device.


Self-Paced Learning

★ Coach Features: Detailed reports measure factors such as progress towards completion of resources and exercises, records on which questions have been answered correctly or incorrectly, and provide a picture of overall understanding on both an individual and full-class level.

★ Customized Quizzes and Exercises: Question sets and exercises can be created in Kolibri in multiple choice and free response format. This customized evaluation tool helps learners practice what they've learned and show what they know, while staying aligned with the goals and standards of their local curriculum.

★ Content Recommendation: Kolibri will recommend educational content to a learner based on the content and exercises they have previously engaged with.
